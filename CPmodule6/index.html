McDonald's is the number one fast food franchise in the world. Their headquarters are in Oak Brook, IL, and their current CEO is Steve Easterbrook (Since 2015). Founded in 1940 by the McDonald's brothers, their gross yearly revenue for the year of 2008 turned out to be a whopper at 23.52 Billion dollars.

Why McDonald's

I have added McDonald's to this app because it is a restaurant at which I frequently purchase food from, and some studies suggest that it is possible to maintain a healthy diet when selecting certain items from their menu which were introduced for consumers who would like to "eat healthy". Among those reasons, coupled with the price at which you can purchase a full meal on a budget, McDonald's is definitely a franchise that will be around for some years to come.
